  • FAQs
  • Where can I buy your dog food?
      A: Our dog food is available for purchase online through our website, as well as through select retailers and pet supply stores. Use our store locator to find a retailer near you.
  • What is your return policy?
      A: We stand behind the quality of our products and offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact us for a refund or replacement.
  • How do I transition my dog to your food?
      A: We recommend gradually transitioning your dog to our food over a period of 7-10 days to avoid digestive upset. Start by mixing a small amount of our food with your dog's current food, gradually increasing the amount of our food each day until your dog is eating only our food.
  • What is the shelf life of your dog food?
      A: Our dog food has a shelf life of approximately 12 months from the date of manufacture. However, we recommend using the product within 6 months of opening to ensure maximum freshness and nutritional value.
